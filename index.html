Our Story

Abby joined a Christian dating app mostly for fun, she wasn’t really expecting much, just curious to see what was out there. Gabe, on the other hand, was ready to delete his profile and move on when, unexpectedly, they matched. Neither of them knew it at the time, but that simple swipe would change everything.

From their very first conversation, it was clear there was something different. They bonded over small things, like their mutual, strong dislike of sushi, and quickly discovered they had a shared sense of humor and values. Messages turned into long chats, and before they knew it, they were eagerly looking forward to their first date.

And now, here they are, excited, grateful, and ready to step into this next chapter with family, friends, and the people who have cheered them on from the very beginning. What started as a “fun experiment” turned into a love story they know was meant to be, and the rest, as they say, is history.
